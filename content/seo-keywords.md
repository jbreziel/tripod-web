# Tripod BV — SEO Keywords

> Keyword map for Dutch and English, per-page targets, and blog topic backlog. This is a launch hypothesis — refine with Search Console + Plausible data after 3 months in market.

---

## Research Approach

Tripod targets **quality-first renovation searches**, not price-first ones. The difference matters:

- "Goedkope aannemer Haarlem" = wrong traffic (price shoppers, high churn, low-quality leads).
- "Aannemer Haarlem ervaring" = right traffic (considered buyers, higher conversion).

Geography: Noord-Holland / Haarlem / Amsterdam metro / Randstad.
Language priority: Dutch primary for domestic homeowners, English secondary for expats.
Intent priority: **informational first** (research-stage), **commercial** (comparison-stage), **transactional** (ready-to-quote) — in that order for blog + educational pages, reversed for service + contact pages.

## Tooling + Cadence

- **Setup:** Google Search Console + Bing Webmaster Tools connected at launch (both languages).
- **Measurement:** Plausible for traffic + search keywords, Search Console for impressions / positions / CTR.
- **Review cadence:**
  - Week 2 post-launch: check indexing status for all pages.
  - Month 1: first ranking snapshot — which queries are we appearing on?
  - Quarter 1: refine per-page keywords based on real query data, prune underperforming targets.
  - Semester: revisit long-tails and update the blog backlog.
- **Refresh trigger:** if a page's primary keyword drops > 5 ranking positions in a month, trigger a content review.

---

## Quick Rules

- Never stuff keywords. One primary per H1, one or two secondaries sprinkled in H2s, long-tails flow naturally in body copy.
- Every page has a unique title + meta description. No duplicates across NL and EN (use hreflang, not duplication).
- Internal links: every service mention in body copy links to its service section. Every project mention links to the project detail page.
- External links: cite sources (government subsidy pages, NEN 2580, architect websites) — good for trust + SEO.
- Image alt text: descriptive, accurate, no keyword stuffing. "Keukenrenovatie met massief eikenhouten werkblad" beats "keukenrenovatie Haarlem badkamer Amsterdam renovatie".
